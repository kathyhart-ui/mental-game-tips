This Week's Mental Cue
"Stop trying to caddy while you’re supposed to be swinging."
Why It Matters

Your caddy brain gathers information: • yardage • wind • lie • target • strategy Your player brain executes the shot. But a lot of golfers mix the two together. They step over the ball still: • questioning the club • debating the target • worrying about the outcome • changing their mind That creates hesitation. And hesitant swings rarely feel free.

Do This On Your Next Shot

Finish the decision BEFORE you step into the shot. Then hand it over to the player part of you.
